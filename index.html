About Me

The Designer Who Decided to Build It.

I graduated with a degree in Information Technology, but my heart initially led me to the creative side of tech. I started my professional journey as a UI/UX Designer, obsessed with user flows, typography, and pixel-perfect layouts. However, I quickly realized that if I wanted my designs to be executed exactly the way I envisioned them, I needed to speak the language of the machine. I didn't get into programming just for the career path, I got into it because I was tired of seeing my UI designs lose their "magic" during the hand-off to developers!
